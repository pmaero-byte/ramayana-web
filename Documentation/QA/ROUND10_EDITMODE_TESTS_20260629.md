## The 5 tests

1. **`CorpusData_LoadsFromResources`** — `corpus_data.json` loads from `Resources/`, parses as JSON, `schemaVersion == "1"`, has ≥13 characters + 8 acts
2. **`CorpusData_CharacterIdsAreUnique`** — `StoryEngine.cs` indexes by `characterId`; duplicates would silently overwrite entries
3. **`CorpusData_AllCharactersHaveRequiredFields`** — every character has `characterId`, `displayName`, `role`, `color`
4. **`CorpusData_AllColorsAreValidHex`** — every `color` matches `^#[0-9a-fA-F]{6}$` (passed to `ColorUtility.TryParseHtmlString` at runtime)
5. **`CorpusData_EightValmikiKandasPresent`** — 6 canonical kanda `actId`s must be in the corpus (`bala-birth`, `ayodhya-dharma`, `panchavati-golden-deer`, `kishkindha-alliance`, `sundarakanda-leap`, `yuddhakanda-war`)

Honest disclosure: the test asserts 6 of the 8 kandas — Bala/Ayodhya/Aranya/
Kishkindha/Sundara/Yuddha. Uttarakanda is split into 2 acts (`return-ayodhya`
+ `uttara-earth-return`) so 6 kanda testIds + 2 uttara = 8 total acts. The
test validates the 6 canonical ones.
